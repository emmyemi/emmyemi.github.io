Desert-Walker

“It’s…complicated.”

Basic Info

Desert has always been something of a problem child. Impulsive and indecisive, valuing freedom and fun above all else, he’s conditioned himself that he can’t—nor should he want to—help anyone but himself.

Having bartered away his very soul to Hircine in exchange for power and purpose, the dark Khajiit merely wanders, taking on all kinds of work in pursuit of excitement and pleasure.
