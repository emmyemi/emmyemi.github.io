Desert-Walker

“It’s complicated.”

Basic Info

Desert has always been something of a problem child. Impulsive and indecisive, valuing freedom and fun above all else, he’s conditioned himself to believe that he can’t—nor should he want to—help anyone but himself.

Having bartered away his very soul to Hircine in exchange for power and purpose, the dark Khajiit merely wanders, taking on all kinds of work in pursuit of excitement and pleasure.

Less concerned with right and wrong and more concerned with what feels most fulfilling, Desert often comes across as careless or coldhearted, particularly towards those who provide little of value to him. At times, he may even prove to be hypocritical, holding others to standards he’d not apply to himself.

Often, Desert looks down upon others, only seeing true value in those who entertain or provide for him in some way, and in order to get what he wants from those around him, he won’t hesitate to go against his word.

In his comfort zone, Desert is frisky and outgoing, content to tease others for his own amusement so long as there’s amusement to be had. While some humanity and genuine love still exists beneath his exterior, none could be blamed for being too put off by Des’ actions to bother looking for it. At the slightest hint of emotional attachment, he tends to create distance between himself and others, and won’t communicate his heart’s own desires.
