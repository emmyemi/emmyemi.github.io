Azharr

Warrior-Priest of the Twin Moons

Basic Info

Born and raised in the heart of Elsweyr, Azharr considers himself about as Khajiiti as one could reasonably get. Azharr is a prideful creature, possessing a strong will, a fiery soul, and a powerful desire to fight and protect.

Once meant to become leader of his small clan, Azharr now wanders Tamriel running contracts within the notorious Fighters Guild, often making only just enough to get by—and drink a little on the side.
